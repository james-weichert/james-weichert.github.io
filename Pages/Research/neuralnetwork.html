FIFA World Cup Match Prediction

james-weichert.github.io/neural-network/

As part of the Harvard Introduction to Data Science class, I worked with two Harvard undergraduate students to build an artificially-intelligent neural network to predict the outcomes of matches at the 2018 FIFA World Cup. Our research found that it is possible to predict the outcomes of World Cup matches approximately 57%-60% of the time, which are accuracies that match or even beat Las Vegas bookmakers when it comes to predicting the winner of an individual match.
