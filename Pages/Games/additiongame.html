Addition Game

For Kindergarten Mathematics Learning

This game, created in a 10th grade product design class, was intended as an educational toy for kindergardeners at my local preschool. The toy features an interal Arduino circuit and multiple wooden blocks. When a student places the blocks in an order that creates a valid equation (e.g. 2 + 3 = 5), the circuit recognizes the values on the blocks and signals a celebration sound. When the equation is incorrect, the toy asks the student to try again. The toy is intended to familiarize students with basic addition and subtraction along with their formal mathematics education.
