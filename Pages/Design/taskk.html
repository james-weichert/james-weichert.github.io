TASKK

Proof of Concept

TASKK is a scheduling assistant designed for students in high school and college. Users can input their class schedule and extracurricular commitments and can select their preferred time for studying. TASKK arranges a study schedule for tests and other assignments by prioritizing based on due date, amount of time needed, and class priority. TASKK takes the hassle out of creating a study plan and maximizes a student's time spent studying or working on an assignment.
