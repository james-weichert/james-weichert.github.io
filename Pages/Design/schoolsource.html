School Source

james-weichert.github.io/school-source/ (Currently Offline)

Created as part of the George Mason High School 2018 Hackathon, SchoolSource is a website that allows students to 'school-source' their academic questions. Student questions are answered by other students while teachers can see what questions are being asked so they can better tailor their lesson plans to students' learning needs.
