George Mason High School Athletics Hall of Fame

www.masonathleticshof.com

The Mason Athletics Hall of Fame is the official home of the records and accomplishments of athletes and teams for George Mason High School in Falls Church, VA. The website has digitized hundreds of records, championships, and inductees that were previously displayed outside of the George Mason gymnasium. All records and inductees are stored in the website’s database, allowing for the dynamic updating of the website and the expansion of the inductee and championship lists later on using the website’s upload portal.
