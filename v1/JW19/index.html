Hi, I'm James.

I'm a High School Student with a passion for Design, Programming, and Entrepreneurship.

Early on, I discovered my love for designing and creating products and digital experiences. I've grown my skills by developing applications and programs in various programming languages with an emphasis on Front-End Development.

Coming from a Do-It-Yourself and Learn-It-Yourself background, my programming style combines industry standards with a general understanding of computer science and design, along with my own set of tricks, shortcuts and workarounds. Particularly, my work focusses on finding the intersections between technology and everyday life, whether through applications, physical products, or a particular design protocol.

Nowadays, I find myself eager to learn a new programming language or discover a new design convention. And while it may not always be easy to find the time to create new digital experiences with my busy schedule, I try to seize every opportunity to use my skills to elevate my life and the lives of others.
